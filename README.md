A Plants vs Zombies-style tower defense game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

## How to Play

1. **Collect Suns**: Click on falling suns or suns produced by sunflowers to earn currency
2. **Plant Defenders**: Click on seed packets to select a plant, then click on a grid cell to plant it
3. **Defend Your Garden**: Stop zombies from reaching the left side of the screen!
